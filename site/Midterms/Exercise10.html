ANIME MANGA GAME REFERENCE

MY FAVORITE THINGS

GINTAMA

by: Hideaki Sorachi

A Japanese manga series with a anime adaptation spanning over 300+ episodes A samurai and his quirky band of friends take odd-jobs in an alternate version of feudal Japan that has been conquered by aliens. Gintama does a bit of everything that is why I like the show. Comedy, Action, Drama and romance all of this genres the show does well. Highly Recommend watching though it may have a slow start.

The Quintessential Quintuplets

by: Negi Haruba.

The series follows the daily life of a high school student Futaro Uesugi who is hired as a private tutor for a group of quintuplets, Ichika, Nino, Miku, Yotsuba and Itsuki Nakano, who perform poorly academically. At the very beginning of the story, it is indicated Futaro will marry one of the Nakano quintuplets but the identity of the bride is unknown until near the end of the story. The Manga has 14 Volumes and 122 Chapters.
