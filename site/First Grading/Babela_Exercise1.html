The Devil is a Part-Timer!

by: Satoshi Wagahara

The Devil Is a Part-Timer!is a Japanese light novel series written by Satoshi Wagahara,
with illustrations by Oniku (written as 029). The story is about Satan seeking to conquer the world of Ente Isla,
but when confronted by the Brave Hero Emilia, he is forced to retreat through a
gate that transports him to modern day Tokyo, Japan. To survive and
find a way to return to Ente Isla, Satan adopts the name Sadao Maou
and gains part-time employment at a fast food restaurant called
MgRonald's, a parody of McDonald's.
